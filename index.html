GRAPHIC DESIGN

A graphic designer is a creative professional who uses visual elements to communicate messages. They design logos, advertisements, websites, product packaging, and other visual content using tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, or Canva. Graphic designers combine art and technology to capture attention, tell stories, and promote brands effectively.

WEB DESIGN

A website designer is a creative professional who designs the layout, visual appearance, and usability of websites. They focus on creating aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly interfaces using design tools and web technologies like HTML, CSS, and sometimes JavaScript. Website designers work closely with clients or developers to bring digital ideas to life, ensuring that websites are responsive, visually engaging, and aligned with brand identity.

WEB DEV

A website developer is a tech professional who builds and maintains websites. They work with coding languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create user-friendly and responsive sites. Website developers may specialize in front-end (what users see), back-end (server-side logic), or full-stack development (both). They collaborate with designers, content creators, and clients to bring digital ideas to life.
